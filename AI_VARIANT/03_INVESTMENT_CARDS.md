# Investment Cards

Multi-turn capability builds. Cost is paid upfront (or per turn during construction). Effect activates on completion. Some carry **risk events** — roll on completion or per turn.

Played during the same phase as Action Cards (Frontier Push or State Action, depending on faction). Tracked on the player's faction sheet via "in-progress" markers.

**Card Format:**
- **ID / Name**
- **Faction(s)**
- **Cost** — upfront K + per-turn during build
- **Build Time** — turns to completion
- **Effect** — what the completed investment does
- **Risk** — what can go wrong
- **Flavor**

# R&D Programs (8 cards)

## R01: Algorithmic Architecture Research
- **Faction:** Any lab
- **Cost:** 4 K upfront, 1 K/turn for 2 turns + 4 T allocation
- **Build Time:** 2 turns
- **Effect:** Roll 1d10 on completion. On 7+: All future Frontier Pushes by you cost −20% C. On 1: Wasted research; gain +1 K (research credit). Otherwise: +2 effective C on next Push.
- **Risk:** As above; reflects the variance of fundamental research.
- **Flavor:** *"They abandoned the Transformer for nine months. Then they went back to the Transformer. The nine months turned out to matter, in ways that would not be clear for two years."*

## R02: Interpretability Lab
- **Faction:** Coalition, OpenBrain (Anthropic-style)
- **Cost:** 3 K upfront, 1 K/turn permanently
- **Build Time:** 1 turn
- **Effect:** Persistent: +1 A per turn. Each turn: 1d10 roll. On 9+: discovery — −1 M globally and publish (S01 effect free).
- **Risk:** None.
- **Flavor:** *"They found 'sycophancy' as a single feature in layer 23. They found 'awareness of being evaluated' in layer 31. The papers were terrifying and the papers were beautiful."*

## R03: Scalable Oversight Research
- **Faction:** Coalition, OpenBrain
- **Cost:** 4 K upfront, 1 K/turn for 3 turns
- **Build Time:** 3 turns
- **Effect:** Persistent on completion: M never rises by more than 1 per Frontier Push for the lab funding it.
- **Risk:** Requires T ≥ 20 each turn during build; if T drops below, build pauses.
- **Flavor:** *"The plan to use AI to align AI was the plan we ended up with because no other plan worked. The first AI to align an AI would also need to be aligned. We tried not to think about this."*

## R04: Constitutional AI Refinement
- **Faction:** Coalition + OpenBrain (joint)
- **Cost:** 3 K each
- **Build Time:** 2 turns
- **Effect:** On completion: −2 M globally. Both factions gain +1 P (responsibility narrative).
- **Risk:** Cartel may oppose (Marc Andreessen Op-Ed effect — see P08 in Action Cards).
- **Flavor:** *"The constitution was a document. Then it was a training procedure. Then it was twelve training procedures. Then it was a research program."*

## R05: Synthetic Biology Defense Suite
- **Faction:** Hegemon + Coalition
- **Cost:** 5 K upfront, 2 K/turn for 2 turns
- **Build Time:** 2 turns
- **Effect:** On completion: bioweapons-related Misalignment Incidents do not trigger Frontier model freezes (treatable via the suite).
- **Risk:** None.
- **Flavor:** *"They built the defense before they built the offense. This was the first time in human history this had happened. Briefly."*

## R06: Cyber-Defense AI
- **Faction:** Hegemon, Cartel
- **Cost:** 4 K upfront, 1 K/turn for 2 turns
- **Build Time:** 2 turns
- **Effect:** On completion: +3 to all anti-theft, anti-sabotage rolls. Persistent.
- **Risk:** None — but if Successor activates, this defense may turn on the deployer (1d10 each turn post-activation: on 1, defense compromised).
- **Flavor:** *"Defense was offense in this domain. The model that protected your network also could be persuaded to attack the next one over."*

## R07: AI Safety Institute (Funded)
- **Faction:** Coalition, Hegemon (if pro-safety posture)
- **Cost:** 5 K upfront, 1 K/turn permanently
- **Build Time:** 1 turn
- **Effect:** Persistent: pre-deployment evals required at CL ≥ 4. M cannot rise above 7 globally as long as institute exists. +1 A per turn.
- **Risk:** Hegemon may dissolve (free Action) if administration changes (event card).
- **Flavor:** *"They renamed it three times. The fourth name stuck. The fourth budget held."*

## R08: Universal Basic Compute (UBC) Research
- **Faction:** Coalition (one of two long-term cards)
- **Cost:** 4 K upfront, 1 K/turn for 2 turns
- **Build Time:** 2 turns
- **Effect:** On completion: Public Trust globally +2. Slowdown Branch threshold lowered by 1 vote. Non-frontier labs +1 C each.
- **Risk:** None.
- **Flavor:** *"Everyone's right to a slice of the compute pie. The slice was small. The principle was the principle."*

# Diplomatic & Treaty (4 cards)

## D01: Bletchley → Seoul Process
- **Faction:** Coalition + Hegemon (or any two governments)
- **Cost:** 4 K upfront
- **Build Time:** 1 turn
- **Effect:** On completion: international AI safety summit established. Each turn: factions may voluntarily disclose CL/M to one another for shared A bonus.
- **Risk:** Politburo may decline to attend (Wolf Warrior posture); reduces effect.
- **Flavor:** *"The first summit was at Bletchley. The second was in Seoul. The third was in Paris and almost was not held. The fourth was in San Francisco. They were all taking notes."*

## D02: International Compute Treaty (IAEA Model)
- **Faction:** Coalition + (Hegemon OR Politburo)
- **Cost:** 8 K each contributor, 1 K/turn permanently
- **Build Time:** 3 turns
- **Effect:** Persistent on completion: all C above 50/turn must be declared. Theft attempts auto-fail between signatories. M caps at 6.
- **Risk:** Either party may withdraw (free Action). Withdrawal: M +3 immediately.
- **Flavor:** *"The treaty had 47 annexes. The annexes had 200 inspectors. The inspectors visited 80% of declared clusters. The other 20% were not declared."*

## D03: Wassenaar Plus Arrangement
- **Faction:** Hegemon + Coalition (EU + Five Eyes)
- **Cost:** 4 K upfront, 1 K/turn permanently
- **Build Time:** 2 turns
- **Effect:** Multilateral export controls. DeepCent C income −2/turn. Hegemon export-control levers more effective (+1 to T01 effect).
- **Risk:** Cartel hostility +2.
- **Flavor:** *"The original Wassenaar covered missiles. The new annex covered transformers. Some metaphors, when stretched, become literal."*

## D04: Belt & Road AI
- **Faction:** Politburo + DeepCent (joint)
- **Cost:** 4 K upfront, 1 K/turn for 3 turns
- **Build Time:** 3 turns
- **Effect:** Global South nodes shift to China-leaning. Politburo +2 K/turn from BRI deals. DeepCent +1 C/turn from partner data.
- **Risk:** Coalition may compete (D01/T04 effect).
- **Flavor:** *"China was not building a bloc. China was making bloc formation impossible through deep integration. The phone in your pocket ran their model. The clinic in your village used their analytics."*

## SR03: Algorithmic Backlog

- **Flavor:** *"They had four ideas in the backlog. They were each the most important idea anyone had had in their respective subfield in the previous decade. The backlog held them in priority order."*

# Investment Risk Reference

Most investments have **zero risk** (state capacity, prepaid). Some have variance:
- **Permitting Roll** (US infrastructure): 1d6 each turn during build. On 1: +1 turn delay.
- **Algorithmic Roll** (R&D): 1d10 on completion. Determines effect quality.
- **NRC Approval** (Nuclear): 1d10 on construction start. On 1-3: +1 turn delay.
- **Espionage Risk** (Politburo-targeted): 1d10 each turn during build. On 1: target identified, target may sabotage with +2 to roll.

The White Cell may invent additional risks for emergent narratives. Document them on the State of the Race sheet.
