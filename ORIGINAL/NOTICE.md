### Component photographs

Photographs of the physical boxed-game components (board, cards, chips, procedure
placemats) may be placed  in `web/apps/frontend/public/classic-assets/` to serve
as an in-app reference (Components gallery + board/placemat views). These are the user's
own photos of a game they own, used for personal play. 


This is distinct from **Hedgemony: Takeoff** (the `AI_VARIANT/` content), which is
an original, transformative work and is not subject to this notice.
