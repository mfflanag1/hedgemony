# Original Hedgemony — Sample Cards

> The only cards printed in the free rulebook (Figures 7.1–7.6, pp.16–18). The full
> decks are boxed-game components and are NOT reproduced. Local study use only —
> see [NOTICE](NOTICE.md). These define the on-card template the White Cell adjudicates.

## PRC-01 — Action (PRC): "Gray Zone Pressure on INDOPACOM: non-U.S. ally"

- Resolution: **use RT B**. Critical Capabilities: None.
- Outcome table (Result → PRC IP / US IP):
  - PRC major gain → +3 / −2
  - PRC minor gain → +2 / −1
  - Status quo → 0 / 0
  - US minor gain → −1 / +1
  - US major gain → −2 / +2
- If unopposed: US −1 IP, and PRC resolves on the Red-Advantage column.
- Side effect: PRC −2 on all regional economic-development die rolls for 1 turn.
- May play every turn. **Cost: 3 RP.**

## PRC-17 — Action (PRC): "Silk Road Economic Belt"

- China seeks global influence via SREB infrastructure between China and Eurasia.
- Attempt to partner with Russia — **Roll D10**:
  - 0–4 → no change
  - 5–7 → minor success: PRC +1 IP & +1 RP, US −1 IP
  - 8–9 → major success: PRC +2 IP & +2 RP, US −2 IP
- May play every turn. **Cost: 3 RP.**

## PRC-22 — Investment (PRC): "SCS Expansion: Reclaim and occupy another SCS island"

- **Roll D10**:
  - 0 → no change
  - 1–7 → completed in two turns (+1 IP)
  - 8–9 → completed in three turns (+1 IP)
- May play every turn. **Public.** **Cost: 4 RP.**

## EVT-PRC-02 — Domestic Event (PRC): "Research Failure (LRF)"

- Rocket-motor test problems delay China's next-gen LRF program.
- Effect: **cannot upgrade LRF for two turns.**
- Visibility: Roll D10 — 0–3 Private, 4–9 Public.

## EVT-PRC-08 — Domestic Event (PRC): "Research Success (C4ISR)"

- Space-research progress aids a C4ISR upgrade.
- Effect: **C4ISR upgrade cost reduced by 2 RP on next attempt.**
- If private, remains private until the modernization public/private die roll it is
  applied to.
- Visibility: Roll D10 — 0–3 Private, 4–9 Public.

## EVT-CON-01 — International Event (Conflict): "Finding Kony" (AOR: AFRICOM)

- After the US/Uganda search ends, Kony returns; the LRA renews attacks across CAR,
  Uganda, and the DRC. Players involved: **US**.
- The US may spend up to 4 RP to support Ugandan forces:
  - **Roll D10** each turn for four turns or until Kony is found:
    - 0–8 → search unsuccessful
    - ≥9 → Kony found, US +2 IP
  - US gets **+1 die-roll modifier for each RP spent beyond the first**.
- If the US does not spend at least 1 RP: US −1 IP.
- **Public.**
