## RP cost categories

- **Deployment** — moving FFs between AORs (and the special CONUS-sourced U.S.
  deployment calculation). Cost scales with distance/AOR crossings.
- **Procurement** — buying new FFs (Table 13.1).
- **Modernization** — raising a force's Mod Level or a Critical Capability Mod Level
  (Table 13.2).
- **U.S. Readiness** — per-turn sustainment + buy-back costs (U.S. only; Tables 14.1–14.4).
- **Card costs** — printed on each card (e.g. sample cards cost 3–4 RP).

## MVP note

Costs are applied by the White Cell (the console has a tracker editor to debit RP and
adjust Mod Levels / readiness). Automatic cost calculators are out of scope for the MVP.
